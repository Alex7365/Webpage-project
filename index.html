Interesting facts about rabbit

Here are ten interesting facts about rabbit

  1. They belong in a family called leporidae

    2. This family include hares

  2. rabbits can jump up to 4 feet

    3. rabbits can jump very high. It is important for them to jump and run from animals such as hawks, lynx, and foxes who are trying to catch these rabbits .

  3. A female rabbit is called a doe, a male rabbit is called a buck, and a baby rabbit is called a kit

    4. These are the names that are similar to deer

  4. rabbits are social creatures that like company and will get sad if they are alone

    5. rabbits usually live in groups, they will groom each other and collect food.

  5. One way that rabbits show they are happy is by shaking their heads and flicking their feet in the air

    6. This is also called a binky. It means the rabbit is happy and energetic.
